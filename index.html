About Our Second Stream

Our Second Stream was entitled "How students may make positive use of Facebook." basically it was all about informing others especially students about the benefits of Facebook.

The Things well done during our stream.

  • AUDIO - Though, we had a slight problem at the beginning as the one mic wasn’t that audible, we were still able to fix it by improvising and just using the other mic that we had.

  • CAMERA - We positioned our camera in a well good place where the whole members would fit in the frame. so, Allthroughout the stream we didn’t have any problems with the camera as everyone was clearly seen the whole time.

  • TRANSITIONS - We were able to test and conduct a dry run with our equipment to ensure optimal functionality.

  • FOLLOWED THE STREAM OUTLINE - We practiced and prepared our flow earlier, so we were able to follow our stream outline.


    • Everything with the equipment we have operated well. Compared to our previous stream, we had a better camera quality that worked much better, and our microphone worked well and was more audible.

    We fully explained our topic, provided the audience with new information for them to learn something new, and further enhanced their understanding of the topics we covered, based on their comments and feedback stating that they understood our topic well. Besides that, we managed to follow according to our stream outline. In contrast to our previous stream, where we spoke about our topic too quickly, and based on our latest stream we discussed and expressed our opinions about it more slowly and appropriately.
